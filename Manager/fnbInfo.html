Description

Caramel popcorn is a delicious treat made by coating popped popcorn with a sweet and buttery caramel sauce. The popcorn is typically air-popped or popped in oil, and then coated with a mixture of caramel sauce and butter, often with a hint of vanilla for flavor. The caramel sauce creates a crispy, sweet coating on the popcorn that is crunchy and satisfying. Caramel popcorn is a popular snack that is enjoyed by people of all ages, and is often served at movie theaters, carnivals, and fairs.
